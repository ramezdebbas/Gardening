Gardening Directions:
Description:
Gardening Directions is an app where you can find the best and most useful methods about gardening, the most useful type of gardening and the descriptions of them as well. This app tells us how you can green your garden.

Features:
•	Fully research based app on How to Green you Gardening.
•	The Research results on the How to grow roses into your garden successfully.
•	Fully Different User Interface Designed keeping in focus point of view of Niche (Target) Market.
•	 Live Tiles(Locally & Remotely)
•	Searching feature.
•	Fully Unique Branding (Unique Logo Designing & Graphics Designing).
